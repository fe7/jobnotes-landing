JobNotes

JobNotes is a candidate focused job application tracking tool, where you can record your notes and thoughts on 6 core metrics per job application that are amongst the top reasons why people accept or decline a job offer.

Save your progress

Save notes on muliple jobs and view them on a dashboard. Green bar next to job names highlight your progress so you get an overview in a glance.

Compare

Compare two job applications and based on your ratings per job. Helping you visualise your choices to make a more informed decision.
